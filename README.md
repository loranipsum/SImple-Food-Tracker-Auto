# Simple Food Tracker Auto (Google Sheets Integration)

A lightweight food tracking application that automatically logs your meals into a Google Sheet stored in your Google Drive. On first use, the app creates a structured spreadsheet, and every subsequent entry is appended in real time—making it easy to maintain a persistent, organized food log without manual effort.

## Features

- 📄 Automatic Google Sheet creation in your Drive  
- ➕ Seamless logging of food entries (date, time, items, etc.)  
- 🔄 Continuous updates to the same sheet  
- ☁️ Cloud-based storage with Google Drive  
- ⚡ Minimal, fast, and easy to use  

## Why Use This?

Perfect for anyone who wants a simple, no-frills food logging system with the flexibility and accessibility of Google Sheets.
